# Teaching and Supervision

My teaching activities at Ulm University cover surface and interface chemistry, electrochemistry, physical chemistry, and practical data-focused skills in modern research workflows.
Furthermore, I supervised several students from Bachelor to PhD involved in projects that I coordinated or am involved in.

Aside from that I am involved in and lead projects that aim at adapting the curriculum to meet the requirements of rapidly changing society with respect to technological developments.

(current-rdm-related-projects)=
## Collaborative Data Literacy Projects

* ["teaching incubator" FDM-LIN](https://www.uni-ulm.de/universitaet/profil/leitbild-lehre/ulmer-lehrinkubatoren/) (funding from Ulm University), which involves the implementation of electronic lab notebooks (ELN) in the curriculum of engineering and natural sciences (October 2024 - September 2026).
* Exchange of ideas and resources within [NFDI4Chem](https://nfdi4chem.de/) (October 2025 - September 2029) to develop strategies for the implementation of data literacy in the curriculum.
* Interdisciplinary experiments in advanced practical courses in chemistry and chemical engineering, funded by the ["Fond der Chemischen Industrie"](https://www.vci.de/presse/pressemitteilungen/fci-foerderung-data-science.jsp), where sample synthesis, functionalization, analysis, and application are conducted by different groups in different courses while using the same ELN (April 2026 - March 2029).
